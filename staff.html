The Staff Archives

Those who teach what should not be remembered...

Professor Morpheus Liddell

Claims to walk through student's dreams at night. Always knows what you dreamt, even if you don't.

Dream Interpretation

Keeps an hourglass filled with sand that flows upward; whispers lecture notes into sleeping ears.

Once marked a student "Absent" before they even skipped class.

Dr. Lyra Kepler

Studies cosmic dust and swears each particle contains a memory of someone long gone.

Stardust Physics

Talks to the Van de Graaff globe in a language no one understands.

Students report hearing their own voices answer her questions... even when they stayed silent.

Curator Tinker

Oversees inventions that were never finished... yet they twitch on shelves as if waiting.

Abyss of Oblivion

Wears nine ticking watches that are never in sync; his pockets jingle with pieces of machines that no longer exist.

If he stares long enough, abandoned projects begin to whisper how they were supposed to end.

The Archivist

Appears only to those who pass the quiz. Their face is always blurred, like an unread line.

Hidden Basement - The Unremembered Syllabus

Speaks in citations, pages turning untouched; words vanish as soon as heard.

Some say the archivist is a window's reflection; others, that the reflection speaks first.

The School of Forgotten Subjects

A place hidden between memory and myth.

Where lessons no longer taught still echo in the halls.
